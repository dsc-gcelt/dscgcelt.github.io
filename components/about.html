About Us


Developer Student Club GCELT is inspired by Google Developers Family. We started our journey in Feb 2019. We try to engage student developers through our hack events, codelabs and meetups. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & hackers in and around the Campus. The technology awareness is main goal for first few years of the group.


Google collaborates with university students who are passionate about growing developer communities and support them with starting student clubs on their campuses. DSC GCELT is the official Google recognised student clubs at GCELT.
